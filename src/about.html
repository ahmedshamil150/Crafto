About Crafto

Crafto is a Pakistani e-commerce store dedicated to celebrating the rich tradition of handcrafted home décor and handicrafts. Every piece in our collection is made by skilled artisans across Pakistan — from the vibrant pottery of Multan to the intricate woodwork of Chiniot.

Our mission is to connect these talented craftspeople with homes that appreciate authenticity, quality, and culture.
