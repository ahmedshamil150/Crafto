Our Story

Built on a Love for Craftsmanship

Crafto was born from a simple belief: that the handcrafted traditions of Pakistan deserve a global stage. From the blue pottery of Multan to the intricate woodwork of Chiniot, the colourful ajrak of Sindh to the delicate marble inlay of Lahore — every region of Pakistan tells a story through its crafts.

We work directly with master artisans and local cooperatives to bring you authentic pieces that carry generations of skill and cultural significance. Each item in our collection is a testament to the hands that shaped it.
