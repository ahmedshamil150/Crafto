About Crafto

Crafto is a Pakistani e-commerce store dedicated to celebrating the rich tradition of handcrafted home décor and handicrafts. Every piece in our collection is made by skilled artisans across Pakistan — from the vibrant blue pottery of Multan to the intricate woodwork of Chiniot, and the colourful textile art of Sindh.

Our mission is to connect these talented craftspeople directly with homes that appreciate authenticity, quality, and culture. When you buy from Crafto, you support a local artisan and bring a piece of Pakistan's heritage into your home.
